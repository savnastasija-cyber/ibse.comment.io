About the program

The program combines international business fundamentals with startup entrepreneurship. Students build practical skills through projects, teamwork, and real-world problem solving.

  • Business strategy and international markets
  • Startup ideation, validation, and go-to-market
  • Innovation, leadership, and communication
  • Project-based learning and collaboration

Why choose this program

Key benefits for applicants:

  • Practical focus: learn by doing
  • Entrepreneurial mindset: from idea to execution
  • International perspective: global business context
  • Career pathways: startups, innovation, business development
